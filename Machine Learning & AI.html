What can ML do for my business?
Machine learning is a technology that helps efficiently extract valuable insights from raw data. That simplifies real-time decision-making, promotes early detection of risks, ensures accurate forecasts, and more. Leveraging the power of machine learning enables companies to:

  • Easier to adapt to constantly changing market conditions
  • Improve business operations
  • Gain a better understanding of overall business and consumer needs
To implement the new processes, you will need to hire data scientists and ML engineers (from an AI/ML company as Relevant). Before implementing, you need to evaluate what data you have, how much you have, and what tasks you need to complete: machine learning works best with very large datasets and highly specialized tasks. Then you must answer three questions about your new algorithm:

What data does it use? What is it good at? What should it never do?

That's all. We'll take care of the rest.

How do you make working remotely successful?
Managing a remote team is not easy. But modern agile methodologies allow us to do this due to:
  • Daily stand-ups - we host stand-ups where everyone participates in a video chat and tells you what they are working on this day and the previous day.
  • Weekly demos: we video chat, share screens, show people their work and then talk about it.
  • Weekly retrospectives: we all analyze what went well and what could be improved based on the demonstration.
