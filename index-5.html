Why is multi-tenant architecture the best for SaaS application development?
There’s no straightforward answer. Multi-tenancy is affordable and easy-to-maintain, making it a good starting point for B and C startups. On the contrary, many established businesses prefer single-tenant architecture for its performance, speed, scalability, better security, and extensive customization capabilities. If you are at a loss, let our experts provide you with SaaS development consulting to analyze your case and define the most suitable option for your needs.
How do you make sure my intellectual property stays secure?
At Relevant, we approach the intellectual property of our clients very seriously. Your product will be protected with the following documents:
  • NDA (non-disclosure agreement) to protect sensitive data you share with us
  • MSA (master service agreement) to reduce legal expenses if you are going to work with us repeatedly
  • SOW (statement of work) to specify all the details connected with the project (features, project stages, risks, and so on)
  • DPA (data processing agreement) to specify how the data you share with us will be processed, stored, and protected
We have templates for these documents. All you have to do is to enter your contact details and sign them.
